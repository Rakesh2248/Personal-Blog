When introducing yourself in an interview as a fresher, it's important to highlight your relevant education, skills, and experiences that make you a strong candidate for the position.
 Here is an example of how you could introduce yourself:

"Hello, my name is [Your Name]. I recently graduated from [University Name] with a degree in [Your Degree]. During my time in school, I gained experience in [relevant skills or coursework]. In addition, I completed an internship at [Company Name], where I was able to apply my skills and learn more about the industry. I am excited to begin my career in [industry or field], and I am confident that my skills and experiences make me a strong fit for this position."

Make sure to be concise, confident, and most importantly be honest.

It's a good idea to do some research on the company and position before the interview so that you can tailor your introduction to fit the company and position requirements.
